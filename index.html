Not Adjaruli

Not Adjaruli is an application that tells you whether or not your food is an Adjarian khachapuri or not by using the modern methods of applying Artificial Intelligence to real-world problems.

Description

Inspired by SeaFood Technologies Inc.'s "Not Hotdog" application, we took a similar idea, reshaped it and made the application based on our national food with the goal to popularize it. The application is built on three main technologies: TensorFlow, Keras and React Native.
